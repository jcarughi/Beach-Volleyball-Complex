About Us

Starting on February 21, 2023, Louisiana Beach Athletics has served Baton Rouge, Louisiana as the second largest man-made Outdoor sand sports complex in the United States, and serves hundreds of thousands of players, spectators, and professionals who participate in organized athletics events each and every week.
We will continue to strive for success and look forward to seeing you all here. Visit us at 1221 Seigen Ln. Baton Rouge, LA 70817.
